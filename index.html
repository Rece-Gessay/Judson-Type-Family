Designer: Daniel Johnson

About: The Judson font, designed by Daniel Johnson, is a versatile serif typeface crafted to enhance readability and support African literacy. It includes a rich variety of glyphs and precomposed combinations tailored for African languages using Latin-derived alphabets. Drawing inspiration from classic serif fonts, Judson features well-balanced proportions, a high x-height, and open counters, giving it a modern twist. Judson is designed for excellent legibility, making it easily distinguishable even in its italic version, which is ideal for lengthy texts. Its broad language and script support make it suitable for global applications. The font is particularly well-suited for academic publications, historical websites, classical literature, e-books, corporate reports, and editorial content, making it valuable across education, publishing, history, corporate, and non-profit industries.
